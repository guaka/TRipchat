How to set up NIP-05 verification:
  1. Copy the NIP-05 identifier above
  2. Go to your Trustroots profile settings
  3. Add the identifier to your Nostr profile field
  4. Click "VERIFY" to check if it's working, or "OPEN URL" to check manually
